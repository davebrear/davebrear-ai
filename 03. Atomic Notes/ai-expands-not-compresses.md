Most people use AI to compress information. Rewrite the email faster. Do the requirements in 10 minutes. Repurpose a deck in half an hour.

The real value is in using AI to expand thinking. To give my brain more time on the things that really matter. Opening a chat window and asking AI to generate something from nothing is compression. Pointing AI at a web of my own interconnected thoughts and asking it to stress-test, challenge, and extend is expansion. This is the difference between [[three-levels-of-ai-usage|level one and levels two and three]].

AI is a force multiplier. The question is what I'm multiplying. [[ai-value-is-in-the-process|The value is in the process]], not the output. [[ai-as-infrastructure-for-thinking|AI as infrastructure]] means multiplying thinking, not text.
