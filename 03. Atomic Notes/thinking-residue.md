The richest retrieval modalities grow from people doing real work inside the system. The [[metadata-search|metadata]] I query today exists because I was filing notes for my own navigation from day one. [[the-link-graph|The link graph]] exists because I was connecting ideas as part of my thinking process.

These are thinking residue. And the deliberate engineering to make that residue queryable is what unlocks it: the search tools, the indexer, the traversal engine, the embedding pipeline.

Neither is sufficient alone. Rich content without tooling is a pile of notes. Tooling without rich content is an empty search box. [[the-best-knowledge-layer-is-a-working-environment|The best knowledge layer is a working environment]], and [[build-systems-people-think-inside|building systems people think inside]] is how the residue gets created in the first place.
