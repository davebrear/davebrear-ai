The most capable AI model in the world starts every conversation knowing nothing about me. Intelligence has scaled dramatically. Memory hasn't. [[the-brilliant-colleague-with-amnesia|A brilliant colleague with amnesia]].

At 500 notes, that's a million tokens of raw content. That exceeds even the largest context windows before any conversation happens. How do I get the right 5-10 notes in front of the AI without burning the entire context window on everything else? [[optimising-context-for-ai|Optimising context for AI]] is the engineering challenge.

This is the retrieval problem. And retrieval has more dimensions than most people expect. The [[four-modalities-of-search|four search modalities]] are the answer.
