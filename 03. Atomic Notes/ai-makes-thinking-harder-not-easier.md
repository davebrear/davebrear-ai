title: "AI Makes Thinking Harder, Not Easier"
type: atom
tags:

---

The thing I didn't expect was how demanding co-thinking with AI turned out to be. It requires me to articulate clearly, evaluate critically, and make judgement calls constantly. I do more cognitive work, not less.

AI makes reasoning visible, not effortless. The AI raises the floor of the conversation, but I'm the one doing the thinking. Thinking feels more demanding with AI, not less. AI amplifies both clarity and confusion, so I have to be explicit about what I actually believe.
