title: "Think Alone First"
type: atom
tags:

---

I open my note-taking tool. Create a new note with a single idea. I don't worry about where it fits. Just capture it. Link ideas together over days or weeks. No outline, no structure, just ideas linked to other ideas.

I don't involve AI here. This is my thinking, raw and unmediated. I know there's enough when I can see a chain of 5-10 connected notes on a topic.

The AI never sees a blank page. It sees a web of interconnected thoughts built over days. That's why the output is good. The raw material was always human.
