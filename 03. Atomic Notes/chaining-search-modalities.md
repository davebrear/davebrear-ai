The modalities don't just coexist. They chain.

**Keyword to semantic.** Find a specific note by exact term, then ask "what else in my vault is conceptually related to this?" Precision first, then discovery.

**Semantic to metadata.** Broad concept search returns 15 notes about AI adoption. Filter by type: just the meeting notes. Just the ideas. The concept search casts a wide net. The metadata search sorts the catch.

**Graph to semantic.** Start at a project note. Traverse links. Run semantic search for the same concepts. Compare. The gap between the two is where blind spots live.
