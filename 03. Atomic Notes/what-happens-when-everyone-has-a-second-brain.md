I keep circling this one. Right now, second brains are a competitive advantage because almost nobody has one. The [[the-experience-gap|experience gap]] is enormous. But what happens when the gap closes?

If everyone has a persistent AI collaborator with deep context, what differentiates? Is it the quality of the thinking you feed in? The rigour of your [[firewall-your-thinking|firewalling]]? The density of your [[links-do-the-work|link network]]?

Or does something else entirely emerge that I'm not seeing yet? [[the-credential-problem|The credential problem]] is one angle on this: when everyone has a thinking system, how do you signal capability?

I don't have an answer here. But I think whoever figures this out first has a significant head start.
