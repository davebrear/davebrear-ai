What made the house move survivable? A storage unit. An escrow for our possessions, independent of either house. Decouple the stuff from the structure and moving becomes a logistics problem, not an existential one.

That storage unit is [[the-context-layer|the context layer]]. Independent of any model, in my control, ready to plug in wherever I go next.

The house is swappable. The people and possessions persist.
The model is swappable. My judgement and context persist. [[model-portability|Model portability]] and [[markdown-as-universal-format|markdown as a universal format]] are what make this practical. [[the-wardrobe-metaphor|The wardrobe metaphor]] and [[the-invisible-arrangement|the invisible arrangement]] are complementary angles on the same insight.
