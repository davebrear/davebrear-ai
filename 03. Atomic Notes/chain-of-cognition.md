Chain of Cognition is the method I use to think with AI under [[augmented-cognition|Augmented Cognition]]. The chef stays in charge: I decide what to think about, what counts as evidence, and what conclusion I will defend. AI works under that direction - [[the-freezer-and-the-sous-chef|the sous-chef, not the freezer]]. The simplest expression of the method in operation is [[the-dinner-party-test|the dinner party test]].

The mechanics belong in their own pieces. Each one teaches a craft move. This atom holds the place; the fuller picture builds across the runway.

What it explicitly is not: better prompting. The prompt is a small move inside a much larger practice. [[ai-outputs-are-inputs|AI outputs are inputs]] is the principle that runs through the whole method - the AI gives me something to shape, not something to accept.
