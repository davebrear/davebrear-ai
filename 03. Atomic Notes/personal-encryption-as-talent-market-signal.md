If the individual context layer in a [[context-stack-in-chain-of-cognition|Chain of Cognition vault]] is separately secured - encrypted under a personal key held by the individual - that is more than a technical feature. It is a talent market signal.

The assurance says: your personal cognition is yours. The company can build shared infrastructure around it. The company can benefit from working alongside it. The company cannot extract it.

Market forces will drive this. Top talent will prefer employers that offer the assurance. Employers that do not will lose people to ones that do. Over time, the default moves toward individual ownership of the personal context layer.

The alternative is [[the-vault-extraction-fallacy|extraction anxiety]] - the worry that every note you write is being mined to reduce your own leverage. That anxiety suppresses the behaviour that makes [[chain-of-cognition|Chain of Cognition]] work in the first place. Secure personal context is not paranoia. It is infrastructure for thinking out loud.
