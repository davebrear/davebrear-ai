Our old wardrobes had survived three moves. By the last house they were screwed to the wall just to stay upright. They worked, but they were creaking, and they weren't surviving a fourth trip. Same clothes, completely new storage.

My AI strategy shouldn't be screwed to the wall either. When the model changes (and it will), I need the clothes to move cleanly. The wardrobe is replaceable. The clothes are mine. [[model-portability|Model portability]] is the principle. [[markdown-as-universal-format|Markdown as a universal format]] is the mechanism. [[the-storage-unit-metaphor|The storage unit]] and [[the-invisible-arrangement|the invisible arrangement]] tell the same story from different angles: own the contents, accept that the structure will change.
