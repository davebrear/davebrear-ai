I didn't spend my first session building folders or writing config files. Instead, I asked the AI to interview me. I told it to ask about my work, my pain points, how I capture ideas, what my daily and weekly rhythms look like, and how I prefer to communicate.

I let it go deep. When it was done, it saved what it learned to a file in my folder. That file was my system. My entire system, for now. The seed of the [[the-context-layer|context layer]].

The seed matters. The plant is mine to grow. [[start-with-a-real-task|Starting with something real]] came next, and [[let-structure-emerge|structure emerged]] from there.
