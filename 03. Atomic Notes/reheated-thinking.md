Reheated Thinking is the name I give to the loop where someone types a short prompt into an AI, accepts whatever comes back, and ships it as their own thought. The output has the shape of an argument - headings, bullets, conclusion - but no one cooked it. Someone microwaved a ready meal and plated it.

The label is deliberately specific. AI is an instrument: [[the-instrument-is-not-the-problem|the violin played without skill is grating, the same violin in skilled hands makes music]]. The thing worth attacking is not the tool, it is the loop. AI as a freezer of pre-cooked positions, opened, reheated, served. [[the-freezer-and-the-sous-chef|That is one role the AI can play; the other is the sous-chef]].

The verdict comes in two parts. At best, uninteresting to eat. At worst, dishonest to pass off as your own cooking. The diagnostic that tells cooked from reheated is [[the-dinner-party-test|the dinner party test]].

Reheated Thinking is the named enemy of [[augmented-cognition|Augmented Cognition]]. The whole practice is built to defeat it. The output it produces is [[meaning-shaped-slop|Meaning-shaped slop]] - the specific subset of [[ai-slop|AI slop]] worth attacking.
