Something that would have sounded absurd a year ago: in 2026, the right context paired with a local model on modest hardware can actually beat a frontier model with incomplete context.

Even a small LLM capable of running on a laptop carries distilled human reasoning in a portable file. Pair that with my own rich context and I have something genuinely powerful: offline, private, and entirely in my control.

The quality gap between local and frontier is narrowing fast. The implication is clear: the context layer is the multiplier, regardless of where the model runs.
