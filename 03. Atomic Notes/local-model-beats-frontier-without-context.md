Something that would have sounded absurd a year ago: in 2026, the right context paired with a local model on modest hardware can actually beat a frontier model with incomplete context.

Even a small LLM capable of running on a laptop carries distilled human reasoning in a portable file. Pair that with my own rich [[the-context-layer|context layer]] and I have something genuinely powerful: offline, private, and entirely in my control.

The quality gap between local and frontier is narrowing fast. The implication is clear: [[context-is-the-differentiator|context is the differentiator]], regardless of where the model runs. This is why [[model-portability]] and [[markdown-as-universal-format|markdown as a universal format]] matter so much.
