There's a progression that matters, and most people haven't got past the first level:

1. **AI as search replacement.** Shallow, overhyped, and the [[ai-sceptics-and-altavista|sceptics' target]] is legitimate here.
2. **[[ai-as-co-thinker|AI as co-thinker]].** Stateless but powerful, deepens individual thinking sessions.
3. **[[ai-as-persistent-collaborator|AI as persistent collaborator]].** Contextual, cumulative, a fundamentally different kind of relationship.

[[the-experience-gap|The gap]] between each level is enormous. Large enough that they might as well be different technologies. Most people are at level one. A growing number are discovering level two. Almost nobody has experienced level three.
