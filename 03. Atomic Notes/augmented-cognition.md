Augmented Cognition is the practice of thinking with AI in a way that strengthens rather than substitutes for human reasoning. It is the category I am writing into. [[chain-of-cognition|Chain of Cognition]] is the method I use within it.

The premise is straightforward: AI is an instrument, not a replacement. [[the-instrument-is-not-the-problem|The tool is never the problem; the hands are]]. Used well it sharpens what I already think. Used badly it produces [[reheated-thinking|Reheated Thinking]]. The same AI plays [[the-freezer-and-the-sous-chef|two different roles]] depending on the relationship I have with it.

This is the umbrella under which the public body of work sits. Each piece I publish adds a brick. The archive is the project.
