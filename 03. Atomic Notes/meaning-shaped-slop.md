Meaning-shaped slop is the output of the [[reheated-thinking|Reheated Thinking]] loop. Headings that look like a structured argument. Bullets that look like reasoning. A conclusion that lands with the cadence of one. Underneath: nothing.

It is the specific failure inside the broader [[ai-slop|AI slop]] category. The contour of meaning without the meaning. The shape, not the substance.

The test for it is [[the-dinner-party-test|the dinner party test]]: can the author name the ingredients, describe the method, and explain the substitutions? If not, it is meaning-shaped. What is missing is the cognitive backbone - point, framing, walk-through, conclusion you can defend. That backbone is what makes [[writing-as-a-delivery-vehicle|the vehicle worth carrying anything at all]].
