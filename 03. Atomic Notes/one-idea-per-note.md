The atomic unit. One idea per note, linked to related notes. The method is deceptively simple, but the constraint is what makes it work.

Externalising ideas into small, focused notes reduces working memory load. I stopped trying to hold the full argument in my head and let the network hold it for me. Linking forces me to articulate why two ideas relate, which deepens understanding. [[links-do-the-work|The links do the work]].

The note isn't the output. The connection is. This is the core constraint of the [[zettelkasten-as-thinking-system|Zettelkasten]]: [[the-writing-is-a-byproduct|the writing is a byproduct]] of the thinking that happens in the linking.
