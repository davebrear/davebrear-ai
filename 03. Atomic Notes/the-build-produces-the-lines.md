When the production trail of a piece is visible, the reader can trace shipped lines back to specific decisions made earlier in the build. The opening paragraph exists because of a refactor pivot. The closing thesis carries because of a metaphor swap. A bolded sentence stands alone because an announcing label got stripped.

This is the pay-off of [[chain-of-cognition|Chain of Cognition]] when packaged for sharing. Not just the output, but the receipts. The reader can challenge any link in the chain, follow it back to where the choice was made, and form their own view.

Annotation is the move. After the piece, surface specific lines from it and name what produced each one. The piece becomes self-aware about its own provenance.

Transparency, done in full, has a certifying effect. The reader does not need to trust the author; they can trace the work.
