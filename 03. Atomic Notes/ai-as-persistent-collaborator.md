Level three: AI with persistent context. The AI knows my projects, my priorities, my writing, my positions, my half-formed ideas. When I start a session, it already knows what I'm working on.

The depth I reach in minute five of a conversation is qualitatively different when there's shared history. I don't re-explain context. I don't re-establish my position. I just continue thinking. The AI picks up where I left off, connects what I'm saying now to something I wrote three weeks ago, and pushes the thinking forward. [[continuity-is-the-value|The continuity is what changed everything]].

Almost nobody has experienced this. The [[the-experience-gap|experience gap]] is real: the gap between [[three-levels-of-ai-usage|level two and level three]] is as large as the gap between one and two.
