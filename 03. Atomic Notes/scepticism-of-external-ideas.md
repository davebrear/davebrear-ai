Information provided by an LLM can often sound very credible and still be very wrong, so I have become automatically distrustful until I have made my own sense of what AI provides. [[ai-outputs-are-inputs|AI outputs are inputs]], never conclusions.

This scepticism has naturally extended beyond AI to include what I read and the interactions I have with others. AI trained me to interrogate everything, and that habit stuck.

The irony: AI's unreliability made me a more critical thinker in general. [[firewall-your-thinking|Firewalling my thinking]] started as an AI discipline and became a general habit. [[echo-chamber-steering|Steering against echo chambers]] is the active version of the same instinct.
