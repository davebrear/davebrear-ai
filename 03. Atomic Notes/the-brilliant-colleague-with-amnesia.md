Co-thinking with ChatGPT was genuinely useful, but it had a limitation I didn't fully appreciate until it was gone: every conversation started from scratch. Each session began with ten minutes of re-establishing context, reminding the AI what I was working on, what I'd already decided, what my frameworks were.

It was like having a brilliant colleague with amnesia. This is [[ai-as-co-thinker|level two]]: powerful within a session, but [[continuity-is-the-value|continuity]] is what unlocks level three.

The most capable AI model in the world starts every conversation knowing nothing about me. Intelligence has scaled dramatically. Memory hasn't. Every new chat window is amnesia. [[the-context-bottleneck|The context bottleneck]] is the technical version of this problem. [[the-context-layer|Owning the context layer]] is the solution.
