The AI goes beyond reading faster. It surfaces connections I missed. It challenges assumptions I'd glossed over. It can say "this note about X seems related to that note about Y" when I'd never have made the link myself.

The network gets richer through collaboration. More links mean richer traversals, which mean richer context, which means the AI spots more connections. A [[the-flywheel|flywheel]].

"This note makes the same argument as that one. Should they be linked?" The notes that [[semantic-search|semantic search]] finds but [[the-link-graph|the graph]] doesn't are potential blind spots: connections I should have made but didn't. This is the [[human-judgement-vs-machine-understanding|human judgement vs machine understanding]] delta at work.
