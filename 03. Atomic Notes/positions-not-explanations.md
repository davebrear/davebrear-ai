What I take away from AI interactions now are clearer positions rather than simply clearer explanations. A greater attention to patterns across conversations rather than isolated insights in the moment.

There has been a move away from searching for a good answer and towards developing my stance. [[from-answers-to-pressure|From answers to pressure]].

The shift is from understanding what other people think to knowing what I think. AI accelerated that by forcing me to articulate and defend rather than just absorb. [[authorship-lives-in-judgement|Authorship lives in that judgement]], and [[ai-makes-thinking-harder-not-easier|the difficulty is the point]].
