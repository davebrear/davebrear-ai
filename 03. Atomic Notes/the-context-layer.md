A persistent context layer that accumulates over time, decoupled from any single model. The AI knows my projects, my priorities, my writing, my positions. Stored in [[markdown-as-universal-format|formats I control]]: plain text, structured metadata, no proprietary lock-in.

The context layer is the storage unit. Independent of any model, in my control, ready to plug in wherever I go next. Owning it means I control how context is retrieved: keyword, metadata, graph, semantic, chained however the use case demands.

Owning the context layer means every model switch becomes an upgrade, not a restart. [[model-portability|Model portability]], [[vendor-memory-is-renting-furnished|not renting furnished]], and [[compound-interest-on-thinking|compound interest on thinking]] all depend on this ownership. [[the-storage-unit-metaphor|The storage unit metaphor]] captures it: decouple the stuff from the structure.
