The value isn't in the responses. It's in the continuity. An AI that knows my files, remembers my conventions, and builds on previous work changes the nature of the collaboration. I stopped prompting and started conversing.

This is what I learned from my first week with Claude Code. The responses themselves weren't dramatically different from ChatGPT. What changed everything was that the AI could read my files directly, remember context across sessions, and build on what came before. It stopped being [[the-brilliant-colleague-with-amnesia|a brilliant colleague with amnesia]].

The leap from stateless to stateful AI isn't incremental. It's categorical. This is the jump from [[ai-as-co-thinker|co-thinker]] to [[ai-as-persistent-collaborator|persistent collaborator]] in the [[three-levels-of-ai-usage|three levels]].
