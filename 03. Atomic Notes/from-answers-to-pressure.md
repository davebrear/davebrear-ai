The shift that mattered most in how I use AI: moving from asking it for answers to asking it for pressure.

Early on I'd arrive with questions to get me started. Now I arrive with existing ideas I want to interrogate. I use AI to argue against myself more than to seek validation. Understanding where an idea breaks and what counterarguments exist deepens opinions and surfaces alternative perspectives. [[ai-makes-thinking-harder-not-easier|It makes thinking harder, not easier]]. A [[rubber-duck-that-pushes-back|rubber duck that pushes back]].

This is the dividing line between [[three-levels-of-ai-usage|level one and level two]]. Most people never cross it.
