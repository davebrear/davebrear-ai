Enterprise pattern: when organisations block or restrict AI access without providing a viable alternative, employees route around the controls. Same pattern as shadow IT a decade ago.

Shadow IT was not solved by blocking harder. It was solved by delivering secure, seamless alternatives. [[the-governance-gap|The governance gap]] is the enterprise version of this problem.
