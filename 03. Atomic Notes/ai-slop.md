AI slop is the catch-all term for low-quality, generic, AI-generated content flooding public writing. I use the term but I am careful with it. In some circles it has come to mean any output an AI was anywhere near, and that is lazy thinking. [[the-instrument-is-not-the-problem|The instrument is not the problem; the hands are]].

The version of AI slop worth attacking is more specific. It is [[meaning-shaped-slop|Meaning-shaped slop]]: output with the contour of an idea and none of the substance. The loop that produces it has a name: [[reheated-thinking|Reheated Thinking]].

Conflating AI slop with all AI-assisted writing buys nothing. It just sorts the audience into tribes before they have read the argument. The whole position depends on the precision: what AI does in the kitchen is fine. What gets served from it is the question.
