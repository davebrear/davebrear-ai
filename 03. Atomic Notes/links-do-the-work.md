When I link one note to another, I'm making a deliberate assertion: these two ideas are connected. The links are doing the work: each one forces me to articulate a relationship between two thoughts.

Over hundreds of notes, these links build into a web of relationships. Starting from a single note, I can traverse outward and load an entire chain of reasoning in one step. A project note links to meeting notes, which link to related ideas, which link to other projects. One starting point, and the full context unfolds.

The chain of thought is visible. I can see how one idea leads to the next, follow the links, and watch an argument form across a network of connected notes.
