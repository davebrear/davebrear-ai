# MOC - How to Start Building Your AI Knowledge System

## The Method
- [[get-interviewed-by-the-ai]] - don't build folders, get interviewed first
- [[start-with-a-real-task]] - bring a real task on day two, let friction guide what to add
- [[the-context-layer]] - give the AI a place to remember you

## The Principles
- [[ai-outputs-are-inputs]] - AI output is raw material, not finished thinking
- [[organise-by-description-not-location]] - describe what things are, not where they sit
- [[let-structure-emerge]] - your first month should look messy
- [[firewall-your-thinking]] - keep your thinking and the AI's visibly separate
- [[build-systems-people-think-inside]] - working environments, not retrieval layers
