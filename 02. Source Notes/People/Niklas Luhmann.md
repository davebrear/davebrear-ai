German sociologist. Published 70 books and nearly 400 academic articles. When asked about his extraordinary productivity, he said he never forced himself to do anything he didn't feel like doing. He just followed his slip box.

Developed the [[zettelkasten-as-thinking-system|Zettelkasten method]].

Source: [[How to Take Smart Notes - Sonke Ahrens]]
