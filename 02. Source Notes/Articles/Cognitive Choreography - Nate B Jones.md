Most people use AI backwards: compressing information instead of expanding their thinking. Jones calls his framework "Cognitive Choreography": sequence models deliberately (voice exploration, then advanced reasoning, then refinement) to develop richer, more original ideas.

> The real competitive advantage comes from optimizing brain time on a subject, not just saving time.
